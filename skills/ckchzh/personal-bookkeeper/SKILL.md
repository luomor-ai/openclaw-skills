# Personal Bookkeeper

Simple double-entry bookkeeping for individuals and freelancers.

## Commands

- `entry` — Add ledger entry
- `invoice` — Generate invoice
- `balance` — View balance sheet
- `profit-loss` — P&L report
- `tax` — Tax summary
- `export` — Export to CSV
