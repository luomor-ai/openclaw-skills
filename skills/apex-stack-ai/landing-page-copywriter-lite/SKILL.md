## Quick CRO Audit (Lite — 3 Dimensions)

### 1. Value Proposition Clarity — /10
Can a visitor understand what you offer in <5 seconds?

**Scoring:**
- 9-10: Headline is specific, benefit-clear, jargon-free
- 5-6: Vague headline, requires reading to understand
- 1-2: No clear value proposition above the fold

### 2. CTA Strength & Placement — /10
Is the desired action obvious and easy?

**Scoring:**
- 9-10: CTA above fold, high-contrast button, clear action verb, repeated on page
- 5-6: CTA exists but weak copy or poor placement
- 1-2: No clear CTA or buried below multiple scrolls

### 3. Social Proof & Trust — /10
Is there evidence others succeeded with this product?

**Scoring:**
- 9-10: Specific testimonials with results, quantified proof, third-party logos
- 5-6: Generic testimonials or just a customer count
- 1-2: No social proof on the page
